Amuri me

When picturing Sicily, one can't help but think about their arancini (rice balls), cannoli, the glistening sea, and the sizzling heat. But there is so much more that makes this beautiful island in the south of Italy so special.

Everything about Sicily, from its food to its architecture, exemplifies the unique nature of its cultural mix and historical heritage. This intriguind land is overflowing with history, nature and traditions, and travellers get lost in the magic of the little moments in between destinations. The idyllic scenery, the charming Baroque backstreets, the palm trees, the brighlty hued boats in the fishing villages. And then the salty air, the local dialect, the street food, the sunlight that slips through the curtains of a window with sea view. But most of it all, nothing enters the heart of visitors more than the warm smile of the locals that welcome you as if you were part of family, as if you have come home.

In the Sicilian dialact, amuri me means my love. It is in this island, packed with charm and magic, that we chose to celebrate together with you our love.

Wedding Events

CEREMONY
  • Noto Cathedral, Noto
  • 13 August 2021, 4PM

In the heart of the historic center of Noto, UNESCO heritage and European capital of the Baroque, the Cathedral of San Nicolò is the most important place of worship in the city. The facade is in the late Baroque style, made of soft limestone with a marked neoclassical note. After the earthquake in the year 1990 and the collapse in 1996 due to a serious construction defect never noticed before in the pillars of the central nave, with the subsequent phase of reconstruction, the interiors have been restored to their original splendor.

RECEPTION
  • Castello Xirumi, Lentini
  • 13 August 2021, 7PM

Among the palm trees and the Mediterranean flora stands the Castle of Xirumi-Serravalle, dating back to the 16th century, with a central crenellated tower embellished by a noble coat of arms. Located in the middle of one of the largest citrus groves in Sicily, the castle benefits from a privileged position just a few minutes from Catania, Syracuse, Caltagirone and Ragusa and about an hour from Taormina. The romantic scenery, in neo-gothic style, will be the setting for our wedding reception.

Travel Information

PLANE

The closest airport to the wedding locations is the Catania-Fontanarossa (CTA) one, about an hour away from Noto and half an hour from Lentini. Other airports on the island are, in order from the closest to the furtherst one, in Comiso (CIY), Palermo (PMO), and Trapani (TPS).

CAR

We recommend to rent a car at the airport to be able to easily reach your accomodation and then drive from the Noto Cathedral to the Xirumi Castle on the day of the wedding.
